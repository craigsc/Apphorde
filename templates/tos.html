    We are AppHorde. We currently have a SDK for Android that allows us to send and display ads on apps that have chosen to integrate our library.     Our service:

    Our service is simple: we make marketing easier for app developers. More specifically, we connect app developers together so that they can cross-promote their apps. By registering and integrating the AppHorde SDK, developers agree to display AppHorde ads on their own app in exchange for their own ads being displayed on the apps of other developers in the AppHorde network. We take care of the hard part, all you have to do is integrate our SDK into your app and you will be ready to go. We also provide real-time statistics so that developers can see how well their ads are performing on the network.
